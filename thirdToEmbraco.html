File Transfer Area

Transfer files from a third-party to Embraco

This page provides an interface for customers, suppliers, and other business partners to send large files to Embraco.
The files will be stored on a temporary location.
Embraco may remove them at any time to conserve disk space or to guarantee the privacy of sensitive data. Embraco will record usage of this system and may monitor its activity.
There is no guarantee of privacy.

Important Notice: You must have authorization to send the file and observe all policies regarding information security and disclosure. Embraco will record usage of this system and may monitor its activity.
