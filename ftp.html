Terms of use:

This file transfer application is intended to allow the transfer of large files from or to Nidec Global Appliance under these conditions:

  1. The files must be connected to the Nidec Global Appliance business
  2. You have been given the proper authorization to transfer the files outside or inside Nidec Global Appliance
  3. The files do not contain illegal material
  4. The application does not guarantee privacy or confidentiality
  5. All transactions are monitored and access may be interrupted at any time.
